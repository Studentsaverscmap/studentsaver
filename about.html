ABOUT US

Save a Student is a mini company aiming to promote our local economy by offering students deals at local businesses. We are a group of four TY students attending Scoil Muire agus Padraig, Swinford, Co. Mayo. We are passionate about shopping local, and partnering with local businesses to offer deals to students is our way to incentivize students to support these businesses.
